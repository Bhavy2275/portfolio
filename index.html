How to add a certificate

Open certificates-data.js and paste a new entry at the top of the certificates array:

{
  id: "course-name-2026",
  title: "Course Title",
  platform: "Coursera",
  issuer: "University or Partner Name",
  date: "YYYY-MM-DD",
  credentialUrl: "https://coursera.org/verify/XXXXXX",
  skills: ["Skill 1", "Skill 2", "Skill 3"],
  imageUrl: ""
},

Save the file, refresh the page — done! Certificates added within 30 days automatically get a NEW badge.
